Our Story

Built by Students,
For Students.

We experienced the struggle of finding safe, affordable housing near our college first-hand. So, we decided to build the solution.

Our Mission

NearNest is a student-first platform designed to bridge the gap between college students and verified landlords. We eliminate brokers, prevent scams, and provide real-time room availability so students can focus on their studies, not their living situation.

The NearNest Standard

  • 100% Verified Landlords
  • Live PostGIS Map Tracking
  • Secure In-App Chat
  • Strict Dispute Resolution
