Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive everything we do

Mission

NearNest bridges the gap between college students and verified landlords. We eliminate brokers, prevent scams, and provide real-time room availability so students can focus on their studies, not their living situation.

Vision

To become India's most trusted student housing platform with complete transparency, safety, and zero brokerage, empowering millions of students across the nation.

What Drives Us

The core values that guide everything we do at NearNest

Trust & Safety

Every landlord is verified with Aadhaar and property proof before listing.

Zero Brokerage

No middlemen, no hidden fees. Students save thousands on broker fees.

College Proximity

Find rooms near your college campus with precise location tracking.
