Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using NearNest.

Is the app really free for students?

Yes! Browsing the map, filtering options, saving favorites, and chatting with landlords is 100% free for students forever.

How are landlords verified?

Every owner must upload their Aadhaar card and proof of property ownership. Our team manually reviews each listing.

Can I search near a specific college?

Absolutely! NearNest uses PostGIS technology to display rooms within a close radius of specific college gates.

What if I spot a fake listing?

We have a strict dispute resolution system. You can report any suspicious room and we investigate immediately.
