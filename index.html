Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using NearNest.

Is the app really free for students?

Yes! Browsing the map, filtering options, saving favorites, and chatting with landlords is 100% free for students forever.

How are landlords verified?

Every owner must upload their Aadhaar card and proof of property ownership. Our administrative team manually reviews and approves each listing before it appears on the map.

Can I search near a specific college?

Absolutely! NearNest uses PostGIS technology to specifically query and display rooms within a close radius (like 2km) of specific college campus gates.

What if I spot a fake listing?

We have a strict dispute resolution system. You can report any suspicious room or user in the app, and our Master Admin Console will investigate and suspend offenders immediately.
