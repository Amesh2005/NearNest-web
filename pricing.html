Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Student Bookings

NearNest is an intermediary platform connecting students with verified landlords. We do not collect rent or security deposits – those are paid directly to the landlord.

2. Service Fee Refund

Since NearNest charges students ₹0 service fee, no refund is applicable. If a future service fee is introduced, it will be fully refundable if the room is not as described or the landlord cancels within 7 days.

3. Landlord Cancellation

If a landlord cancels a confirmed booking, the landlord may be liable for a cancellation fee (up to ₹1000). Students are entitled to a full refund of any amount paid to the platform.

4. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes regarding room quality, deposit, or rent must be resolved between the student and the landlord. NearNest provides a dispute resolution system but is not responsible for the final outcome.

5. How to Request a Refund

Send an email to nearnest.2026@gmail.com with your booking details. Our team will respond within 24 hours.
