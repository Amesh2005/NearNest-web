Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to NearNest. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice or our practices with regard to your personal information, please contact us at nearnest.2026@gmail.com.

When you use our mobile application and services, you trust us with your personal information. We take your privacy very seriously, especially given our student-first approach.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, and passwords when you register as a student or property owner.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we utilize live GPS data to power our Map-Based Discovery (using PostGIS) to help you find rooms or PGs near specific college gates.
  • Verification Data: For owners, we collect Aadhaar and property proof documentation strictly for administrative verification purposes.
  • Communications: We securely process chat messages exchanged between users within the app to facilitate safe communication regarding room availability.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our App for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon processes.
  • To connect students with verified property owners securely.
  • To provide real-time room availability updates via Supabase WebSockets.
  • To handle dispute resolutions through our Master Admin Console.

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, or to protect your rights. For example, a student's basic profile may be visible to an owner when inquiring about a room, and an owner's verified property details are visible to students browsing the map.

5. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. All user verification documents and communications are stored securely and are only accessible by authorized personnel.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this notice or the NearNest platform, you may email us at:

nearnest.2026@gmail.com

Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Intermediary Role

NearNest is a technology platform that connects students and landlords. We are not a party to any rental agreement. We do not own, inspect, or endorse any property unless explicitly marked “NearNest Verified”.

2. No Brokerage

NearNest does not charge brokerage fees to students. Landlords pay a service fee only when a successful match is made.

3. Verification

We perform basic identity and property document checks, but final due diligence is the responsibility of the user.

4. Liability

NearNest shall not be liable for any loss, damage, or dispute arising from a rental agreement between a student and a landlord.

5. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of India, and any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of Nashik, Maharashtra.
